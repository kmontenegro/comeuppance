    Engagement Rates

    Ken Montenegro Published on March 31, 2017

    A retainer equal to 20% of estimated project total is required to initiate work; hourly work is due net-15 days after hours are worked. Payments made after the 15th day are penalized at a reasonable rate.

    Commercial/Not Nonprofit:

      $210/hour unless pricing arranged by contract

    Nonprofit Over $3M Annual Budget:

      $185/hour unless pricing arranged by contract

    Nonprofit Less Than $3M Annual Budget:

      $165/hour unless pricing arranged by contract

    Project/contract scope price:

      Beyond half-hour, $80/hour
