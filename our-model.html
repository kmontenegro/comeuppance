Our Model

    Ken Montenegro Published on March 31, 2017

    We believe that technology is nothing but a lever. Technology is not justice, technology is not equity, technology will not save us. Technology can help us do our work in a more secure, inclusive, and hopefully egalitarian way.

    Our vision is to be a support and resource to organizations and individuals who hope to leverage technology in the way envisioned above.

    Currently, as a consulting practice of one, we must take a boutique approach to clients and, if we aren't a right fit, we'd be glad to recommend someone we know.

    Contact us if you'd like to explore a new and holistic approach to technology, data stewardship, and digital security.
